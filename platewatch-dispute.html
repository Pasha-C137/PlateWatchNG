Review Process
1
Submit DisputeYou fill this form and submit your case.
2
Confirmation SentYou receive an email confirming the dispute was received.
3
Human Review (48hrs)Our team reviews both sides — the original report and your dispute — plus any evidence from both parties.
4
Decision IssuedThe report is upheld, amended, or removed. Both parties are notified.
Possible Outcomes
✅Report RemovedIf the dispute is upheld — the report is permanently deleted.
✏️Report AmendedIf details were inaccurate but the incident occurred — the report may be corrected.
📌Report UpheldIf evidence supports the original report — it remains as-is.
⚠️Disputed LabelIn unresolved cases, the report is labelled "Under Dispute" until resolved.
Important Notice

Filing a false dispute to suppress a legitimate report is a misuse of this platform and may result in your dispute being permanently rejected.

If the report involves a serious criminal allegation such as a hit and run, we recommend also contacting the Nigeria Police Force directly.
